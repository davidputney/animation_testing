Apple Watch App

Getting people to think saving for retirement is a significant challenge for plan sponsors across industries. Motivating people to actively engage in their retirement savings plan? That’s a whole different issue. How did we solve that challenge?

Empower Retirement (Empower) provides retirement services to companies of every size, from the small to mid, and the large to the mega plan. Empower is the second-largest retirement services provider in the country with over 8 million participants on its platform.

Their primary mission goes back to its namesake: empowering people to take their financial destinies into their own hands, or wrist, in this case.

Working with Empower, we identified four aspects of the Apple Watch app that fits with their mission to keep people actively involved in their retirement accounts.
  • Display a user’s progress toward achieving their retirement income goal
  • Show current balances and value of their investments
  • Provide simple notifications that users can respond to from the Apple Watch
  • Make retirement planning playful by displaying a countdown clock that projects how many days remain to the user’s retirement date
